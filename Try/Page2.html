The Program We Provide

(1) Cardio Power Hour


Duration: 4 weeks

Objective: Improve cardiovascular endurance, boost energy levels, and promote fat loss.

Weekly Schedule:

Day 1: Cardio Kickboxing (45 minutes)

A high-energy class that combines cardio moves with kickboxing techniques to get the heart rate up.

Day 2: HIIT Circuit (30 minutes)

A high-intensity interval training session focusing on short bursts of all-out effort followed by brief recovery periods.

Day 3: Dance Cardio (45 minutes)

A fun dance-based cardio workout set to upbeat music to keep participants engaged and sweating.

Day 4: Low-Impact Cardio (30 minutes)

A gentler, joint-friendly cardio class designed for all fitness levels.

(2) Strength and Sculpt


Duration: 6 weeks

Objective: Build muscular strength, tone the body, and improve overall fitness.

Weekly Schedule:

Day 1: Upper Body Strength (45 minutes)

Concentrates on exercises targeting the chest, back, shoulders, and arms using body weight and/or dumbbells.

Day 2: Lower Body Strength (45 minutes)

Focuses on exercises for the legs and glutes, incorporating squats, lunges, and resistance bands.

Day 3: Core Conditioning (30 minutes)

A class dedicated to core exercises, including planks, Russian twists, and leg raises.

Day 4: Full-Body Circuit (45 minutes)

Combines upper and lower body exercises in a circuit format for a comprehensive workout.

(3) Yoga and Mindfulness


Duration: Ongoing

Objective: Enhance flexibility, reduce stress, and promote mindfulness.

Weekly Schedule:

Day 1: Hatha Yoga (45 minutes)

Focuses on basic yoga poses and alignment to improve flexibility and balance.

Day 2: Vinyasa Flow (45 minutes)

A dynamic yoga class that links breath with movement to promote strength and flexibility.

Day 3: Restorative Yoga (30 minutes)

A calming and restorative practice that uses props to relax and release tension.

Day 4: Mindfulness Meditation (15 minutes)

A guided meditation session to cultivate mindfulness and reduce stress.

(4) Functional Fitness Bootcamp


Objective: Enhance functional strength, improve agility, and prepare participants for real-life physical challenges.

Weekly Schedule:

Incorporates ladder drills, cone drills, and other agility exercises.
